DAY 01 RIO DE JANEIRO – FOZ DO IGUASSU

Arrival in Iguassu, reception by our local bilingual guide and transfer to your hotel.

Foz do Iguassu: The wonderful Iguassu Falls formed by 275 waterfalls that can be visited from both Brazilian and Argentinean sides, walking along the pathways over the Iguassu River, in boats that goes almost the foot of the falls or by a breathless helicopter flight over them. The impressing scenery surrounded by its wide sub-tropical vegetation, flora and fauna is certainly an unforgettable image that remains forever in one’s mind.

PM Half Day visit Brazilian side of the Falls Leaving from other hotels on the Brazilian side by motorcoach and crossing Iguassu National Park as far as the Brazilian Falls. Walking along the paths following cascades and then walking along the catwalk that leads very close to the bottom of the Devil's Throat. The guests will enjoy magnificent views. Taking an elevator to go up to the road, and there boarding the motorcoach to get back to the hotel. Important: For clients at Belmont Cataratas hotel, will be provided a guided walking tour.

Half Day Macuco Boat Tour

This incredible acivity called Macuco Safari is divided into three stages: Jungle, Trail and Boat. It all starts with a 2 km journey through the jungle of the Iguaçu National Park, where you will be transported by ecological vehicles powered by electricity that, in addition to being silent, guarantee a panoramic view of the numerous species of vegetation that make up the scenario.

DAY 02 FOZ DO IGUASSU

Brazilian Buffet Breakfast AM: Half Day visit Argentinean side of the falls Leaving from other hotels on the Brazilian side for a drive and a quick customs check, crossing the Tancredo Neves bridge over the Iguassu River, which forms the border between Argentina & Brazil. Once in Argentina, tour takes a scenic highway up the Argentinean Iguazu National Park. Tour starts with ample time to visit Argentinean falls. Stopping at the Visitors' Center and proceeding from there the Superior Circuit, the up-river cascades, to the Inferior Circuit, the down-river falls and then takes the Ecological Jungle Train that leades visitors to and from the the most impressive observation point of the falls - The Devil´s Throat Return to the hotel and National park entrance fee included.
